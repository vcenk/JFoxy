# 13 – Interview Practice: Session Summary

## 1. Purpose

After a practice session, provide:

- Overall score.
- Trend compared to previous sessions.
- Per-question breakdown with detailed cards.
- Smart next-step recommendations.

---

## 2. Layout

Top:
- Title: “Session Complete”
- Export icon (for PDF/DOCX script export).

Scoreboard:
- Big number: e.g. 82/100.
- Underneath:
  - Light sparkline graph of last sessions (no axes).
  - Badge: “▲ +6 pts vs your average on this topic”.

Body:
- List of **expandable cards**, one per question.

---

## 3. Question Cards

Collapsed state:
- Question text (truncated).
- Small difficulty label.
- Score badge (e.g. 7.4, colored).
- 1-line AI summary: “Great structure, missing quantification.”

Expanded state:
- Transcript with highlights.
- KPI mini bars.
- "Improved answer" toggle to show AI’s version.
- Button: “Retry this Question”.

---

## 4. Smart Suggestion Banner

If clear weaknesses identified:

- Display light blue “Coach Insight” card:
  - Text (example):  
    “Your answers lacked concrete metrics. Work on Results in your STAR stories.”
  - CTA:
    - “Open STAR Builder” (if structure weak).
    - “Open Gap Defense” (if gaps or missing experiences).

---

## 5. Footer Actions

Primary:
- “Retry Weak Questions (n)”

Secondary:
- “Start New Practice Session”

Export (top-right):
- Opens a modal with:
  - Download as PDF / DOCX
  - Options:
    - Include transcripts
    - Include feedback
